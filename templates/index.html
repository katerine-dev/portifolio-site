About me!

I am currently pursuing a degree in computer science at Anhembi Morumbi University in São Paulo.

However, this isn't my first academic journey. In 2022, I successfully earned a degree in Law, and it was during one of my internships that I unexpectedly landed into the technology sector. Initially, programming seemed like a daunting challenge, but, after my first programming courses, I quickly realized that it was a skill I could master.

My initial exposure to programming occurred in the realm of legal research, where we utilized the R language for data analysis. This experience marked a turning point for me, sparking a newfound interest in programming.

Outside work, I find solace in reading books, drawing, traveling, and playing with my Nintendo console.
