About me!

I appreciate the beauty of opposites, particularly in complementary colors, such as the harmonious pairing of purple and yellow.

Computer science student

I am currently pursuing a degree in computer science at Anhembi Morumbi University in São Paulo. However, this isn't my first academic journey. In 2022, I successfully earned a degree in Law, and it was during one of my internships that I unexpectedly delved into the technology sector. Initially, programming seemed like a daunting challenge, akin to taming a monster with a formidable reputation. However, as I embarked on my first programming courses, I quickly realized that it was a skill I could master.

My initial exposure to programming occurred in the realm of legal research, where we utilized coding for data analysis. This experience marked a turning point for me, sparking a newfound passion for programming. Today, I consider myself a programming enthusiast, driven by curiosity and a constant desire to deepen my expertise. While I enjoy exploring various facets of programming, my current focus lies in developing REST APIs with Python.

Outside the realm of code, I embrace diverse interests during my "free time." I find solace in reading books, drawing, travel, and unwinding with my Nintendo console.
